Frequently Asked Questions

How long do veneers last?

Porcelain veneers generally last between 10 to 15 years, or even longer, if properly looked after with daily brushing, flossing, and standard clinical dental checks.

Are veneers painful?

No. We use reliable local anahetics to numb your mouth fully during preparation steps, meaning the entire physical clinical treatment process feels comfortable.

How much does a smile makeover cost?

The price is completely dependent on how many teeth need treatment and whether you choose porcelain or composite materials. We provide an exact price quote at your assessment.

How long does treatment take?

Composite treatments take just one day. High-end custom porcelain veneers typically take two sessions spaced out over a couple of weeks while labs design the pieces.

Can I finance treatment?

We provide several secure third-party cosmetic payment plans to help break the final investment figure down into affordable monthly instalments.

Do results look natural?

Yes. Our absolute design focus is building smiles that perfectly match your face shape, skin tone, and lip line alignment so they never look fake or artificial.
